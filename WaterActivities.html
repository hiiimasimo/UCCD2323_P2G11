Sipadan Island

Renowned as one of the top dive spots globally, Sipadan Island in Sabah is a must-visit. Divers can explore its steep walls and encounter turtles, barracudas, and even hammerhead sharks in the deep blue waters.

Redang Island

Known for its pristine beaches and clear waters, Redang Island is a haven for divers. The island's coral reefs are vibrant and well-preserved, making it an excellent location for underwater photography.

Tioman Island

Tioman Island is another gem, offering diverse dive sites ranging from wreck dives to coral reefs. Marine life here includes everything from small nudibranchs to majestic whale sharks.
